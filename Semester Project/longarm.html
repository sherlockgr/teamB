Longarm Quilting Services

Congratulations on completing your quilt top. I am sure hours and hours have gone into your project. I would be honored to help you finish your beautiful quilt.

My longarm edge to edge quilting service is $0.02 per square inch. A square inch is calculated by multiplying the length and width. For example, if your quilt measures 80" length x 60" width = 4800 square inches. 4800 x $0.02 = $96.00.
